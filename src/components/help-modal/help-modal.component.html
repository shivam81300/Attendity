The 75% Rule

Most universities require students to maintain a minimum of 75% attendance in each subject to be eligible for exams.

This app helps you stay on top of this requirement.

How the Calculator Works:

  • If your attendance is below 75%, it calculates the minimum number of classes you must attend consecutively to get back above the 75% mark.
  • If you are at or above 75%, you are in the "Safe Zone".
  • The calculation assumes you will be marked 'Present' for all future classes until the goal is met.

Keep track, stay safe, and ace your exams!
